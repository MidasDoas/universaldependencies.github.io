Aux chain

Auxiliary dependencies should not form a chain.

Search expression: _ <aux (_ <aux _)

Correct example:


Do you think that he will have left when we come ?
aux(think, Do)
aux(left, will)
aux(left, have)

Incorrect example:


Do you think that he will have left when we come ?
aux(think, Do)
aux(have, will)
aux(left, have)

Name is right-headed

Name dependencies should be left-headed, not right.

Search expression: _ <name@R _

Correct example:


Carl XVI Gustaf
name(Carl, XVI)
name(Carl, Gustaf)

Incorrect example:


Carl XVI Gustaf
name(Gustaf, XVI)
name(Gustaf, Carl)
